The Fish lake

Fish Lake is the sixth lake in the group of the Seven Rila Lakes . It is located at 2184 m above sea level, north of Haramiyata peak - at the western foot of the ridge, which descends from the top to the north-northeast. The fish lake is the shallowest of the seven (average depth 1.10 m). Its area is 35 decares and the volume is 38,000 m 3 . It is 250 m long and up to 140 m wide. It is fed by the outflow of Lake Trilistnika (fifth) and drains into the Lower Lake (seventh). The Seven Lakes hut is located along the northeastern shore of the lake . From its end to the west is the path to the new hut Rila Lakes .
